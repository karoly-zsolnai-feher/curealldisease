🩺 Snapshot of AI‑driven breakthroughs

  • 🧩 AlphaFold 3 opens the Lego box
    Predicts how proteins, DNA and drugs snap together, giving scientists a near‑finished puzzle to speed new medicines. Labs call it “Google Maps for molecules”. (Source: Nature 2024 AlphaFold 3, credibility 8/10)
  • 💊 First AI‑designed drug hits Phase 2
    Rentosertib improved lung capacity by ≈ 98 mL in fibrosis patients in a Phase 2a study announced June 2025 - proof that generative models can move beyond the sketchpad. (Source: DrugTargetReview 2025, credibility 6/10)
  • 🧬 CRISPR cures reach hospital wards
    Casgevy, the first CRISPR therapy, now spares sickle‑cell patients from painful crises; prime editing entered its first human test last month, with AI designing guide RNAs in minutes. (Source: FDA 2023 & Nature 2025, credibility 7/10)
  • 🦠 New AI antibiotic enters humans
    Zosurabalpin - a macro‑peptide found by deep learning - began Phase 1 trials this year, the first new class against a lethal superbug in 50 years. Think of it as discovering a fresh spare key for a jammed lock. (Source: Nature 2024, credibility 7/10)
